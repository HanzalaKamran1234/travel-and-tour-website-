Specialized Appointment Services

Premium registrations for official document processing and medical assessments.

Consulate Biometrics

Tasheer Saudi Visa Appointment

Secure your biometric enrollment and physical document verification slots at the official Tasheer visa service centers in Pakistan. Essential for work, family visit, residency, and commercial visas to the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

Required Documentation:
  • Original Passport (Valid for at least 6 months)
  • CNIC / NICOP Copy
  • Visa Approval / Invitation Letter copy
  • Two passport-sized photographs with white backgrounds
GCC Pre-Departure Health

GAMCA / Wafid Medical Appointment

Generate your mandatory Wafid/GAMCA medical slip for Gulf travel. Our system coordinates slots with authorized GCC medical examination boards in Islamabad, Lahore, Karachi, Peshawar, Multan, and other key cities.

Required Documentation:
  • Original Passport & Copy (valid minimum 6 months)
  • Original CNIC & Copy
  • Specific destination details (Saudi Arabia, UAE, Oman, etc.)
  • 4 passport-sized photographs
Vocational Certification

NAVTTC Verification Appointment

Schedule your skills validation assessments and official diploma verification appointments at the National Vocational and Technical Training Commission. Vital for skilled laborers needing attested degrees for official overseas work visas.

Required Documentation:
  • Original Diplomas / Skill Certificates
  • Academic Transcripts & Copy
  • Original CNIC
  • Employer offer letter copy (if applicable)
